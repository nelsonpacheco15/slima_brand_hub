## Social handles

Instagram + Threads share one @ (Threads inherits the Instagram handle). Grab the match on X and TikTok too.

**Pick order:** `@slima` → `@heyslima` → `@slima.writes`
- `@slima` — cleanest; grab if free.
- `@heyslima` — warm, human, on-brand ("hey, Slima").
- `@slima.writes` — literary, active.

Keep the **display name** as `Slima` regardless of the handle. Avoid `@slima_ai` for the public handle (don't lead with AI).

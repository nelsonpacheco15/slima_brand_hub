Common questions

Good to know.

Is there really a free tier? +
Yes. The editor, files, and habit system are free forever. The first time you open the coach you get a seven-day trial, then you decide.
Can I import a draft I already started? +
Drop in a Word, Markdown, or plain-text file. Slima detects chapter breaks, builds the project tree, and picks up where you left off.
How is this different from Scrivener or Google Docs? +
Scrivener is great at structure but has no coach that read the whole book. Docs is great at collaboration but treats every document the same. Slima brings structure, typography, and an in-context coach together for long-form writers.
Will my book be used to train models? +
No. Your drafts are encrypted, never used for training, and never shown to anyone else. Each request reads your work, answers, and forgets.

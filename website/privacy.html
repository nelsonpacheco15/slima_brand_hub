Legal

Your work stays yours.

Last updated June 2026. A plain-language summary of how we handle your writing and your data. Have your own counsel review before relying on it.

The short version

Your writing is private. We do not use it to train writing models, and we do not make it visible to anyone else. It powers your own project and nothing else.

What we collect

We collect the account details you give us, such as your email, and basic usage data that helps us keep Slima reliable. We collect the content you create so we can store it and run the features you ask for.

How we use it

We use your information to run the service, process your writing through the features you use, handle billing, and answer your questions. We do not sell your data.

Where it lives

Your work is stored securely and is available only to you, unless you choose to share it with a team. You can export or delete your projects at any time.

Your choices

You can access, export, or delete your data, and close your account whenever you like. Deleting a project removes it from your workspace.
